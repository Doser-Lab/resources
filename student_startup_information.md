# Miscellaneous Student Startup Information

This is a miscellaneous list of information for graduate students starting in the SEFS Lab. This document should be read within the first couple of days of the beginning of your program. Items listed below are in no particular order. 

+ For out of state residents, you will need to establish NC residency in order to qualify for in-state tuition within your first year. This requires getting your NC driver's license. The NC DMV is notoriously slow, so you must plan ahead in order to get this done ASAP. **One of the first tasks you should do after you move to NC is getting your NC driver's license, otherwise you may not be able to meet the timeline to become an NC resident and qualify for in-state tuition**.
